## About me
![StaccaToe squatting](/images/staccatoe_squat.png){: .align-right width="300px"}
I am a fourth-year undergraduate Mechanical Engineering major at the University of Massachusetts Amherst (UMass Amherst). I am an Undergraduate Student at [DARoS Lab](https://www.umass.edu/robotics/daros)  supervised by Professor Donghyun Kim, where I am developing a custom guide dog robot. I was also an Undergraduate Student at [MRRL](https://www.umass.edu/robotics/mrrl) supervised by Professor Frank Sup, where I integrated robotics and engineering in nursing as a part of the [Elaine Marieb Center for Nursing & Engineering Innovation](https://www.umass.edu/marieb-nurse-engineer/).

I previously worked as a Research Intern at the Robotics Group of [OMRON SINIC X Corporation](https://www.omron.com/sinicx/en/) in Tokyo, Japan, during the summer of 2023. I was supervised by Dr. Kazutoshi Tanaka and Dr. Masashi Hamaya for the [development of a faster shape memory alloy actuator](https://medium.com/sinicx/intern-report-development-of-a-faster-shape-memory-alloy-actuator-513ed66dbdbf).
