I designed a speed control interface for the guide dog robot, Unitree Go 1, used at the Dynamic and Autonomous Robotic Systems (DARoS) Lab at UMass Amherst. This interface features two configurable buttons connected to the robot's onboard computer, ensuring ease of use for blind and low vision individuals. This speed control interface, along with wearable vibration sensors that I designed, was utilized in the Vision Assistance Race of the 2023 [Cybathlon Challenges](https://youtu.be/AoxKgz5U6xg&t=52).
