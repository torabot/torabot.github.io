Under the supervision of [Professor Donghyun Kim](https://www.umass.edu/robotics/people/donghyun-kim) and [Hochul Hwang](https://www.umass.edu/robotics/people/hochul-hwang), I am currently leading a group of five other senior mechanical engineering majors to design a quadruped robotic guide dog for my Senior Design Project. This project aims to solve hardware limitations of current quadrupeds for use as a legged mobility aid for blind and low-vision individuals. This project is a continuation of a previous study [published in CHI 2024](https://arxiv.org/html/2402.06790v1).
