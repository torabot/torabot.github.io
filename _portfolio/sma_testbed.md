During my summer 2023 research internship at the Robotics Group of OMRON SINIC X Corporation in Tokyo, Japan, I worked on the development of a shape memory alloy actuator test bed that utilizes temperature controlled water to actuate nickel titanium shape memory alloy wires. I designed the test bed and created a water switching system using a series of Arduino controlled solenoid valves. At the end of the internship, I was able to assemble the test bed and create a fully functional Arduino controlled water switching system. However, due to time constraints of the internship program, the test bed was not fully finished.
