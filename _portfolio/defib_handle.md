In partnership with the (Elaine Marieb Center for Nursing and Engineering Innovation)[https://www.umass.edu/marieb-nurse-engineer/] and (Baystate Medical Center)[https://www.baystatehealth.org/locations/baystate-medical-center], I designed a novel custom handle for a defibrillator for the Emergency Department & Trauma Center at Baystate Medical Center. This handle is medical grade and can be 3D printed, lowering manufacturing costs significantly.
